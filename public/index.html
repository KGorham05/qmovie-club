About QMC

Quarantine Movie club brings your friends together while you stay apart.

Everybody loves a socially distant viewing party, but pleasing everyone with the perfect flick is not easy. I created QMC for my friends to suggest and vote before our weekly movies, and chat while we enjoyed the show.

We loved our movie club so much, we wanted to be able to make more of our own. Now anyone can start a new club, invite members, customize show times and genres, and get watching with your friends from afar. QMC brought me and my friends a lot of joy. I hope it brings some to you too.

- Kevin Gorham
