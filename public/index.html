Why QMC?

When quarantine started, a small group of friends got together and started having viewing parties once a week. However, it quickly became apparent that it was difficult to decide what to watch as a group.

Quarantine Movie Club was borne of that need. We wanted a democratic way to suggest and vote on movies, and as an aspiring web developer, I took on the challenge of creating a website that would allow us to do just that. Each week we could agree on a theme, and suggest movies to watch. We added a chat room so that our group chat wouldn't annoy those who couldn't make a particular showing.

After a moderate amount of success, people requested the ability to make their own movie groups. As a way to further my develop my skills, I added user authentication, the ability for users to create their own groups, and the result is what you see here.

I hope this simple application allows you and your friends or family to remain connected, and it brings you as much joy as it has given me.

- Kevin Gorham
